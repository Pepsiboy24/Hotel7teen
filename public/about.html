Our Story

The Beginning

Founded in 2010, Hotel7teen emerged from a vision to create an unparalleled hospitality experience in the heart of Kaduna. Our founders, with decades of collective experience in luxury hospitality, envisioned a sanctuary where elegance meets exceptional service.

Our Philosophy

At Hotel7teen, we believe that true luxury lies in the details. From the handcrafted furnishings in our suites to the carefully curated menus in our restaurants, every element is designed to provide an experience that transcends mere accommodation.
